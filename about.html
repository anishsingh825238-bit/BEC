Company Summary

Who We Are

Bhawani Engineering Constructions (BEC) is a premier mechanical engineering and construction company established in 2012 by Mr. Abhay Singh. Headquartered in Shahpur, Sikraur, Shivsagar, Rohtas, Bihar, we have grown to become a regional market leader in industrial construction and engineering services.

With a dedicated workforce of 500+ skilled professionals, we operate across 10+ states in India, delivering excellence in cement plant installation, power plant construction, steel plant erection, and specialized civil works.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and timely delivery has earned us the trust of industry giants like ACC Cements, Dalmia Cements, UltraTech Cements, Ramco Cements, and Larsen & Toubro Construction.

Founder & Managing Director

Mr. Abhay Singh is the visionary founder of Bhawani Engineering Constructions. With deep expertise in mechanical engineering and construction management, he established BEC in 2012 with a mission to provide world-class engineering services to India's growing industrial sector.


Under his leadership, BEC has grown from a small regional contractor to a PAN India engineering company with 500+ skilled employees, serving major clients across 10+ states. His emphasis on integrity, quality, and timely delivery has been the cornerstone of BEC's success.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Objective

01

Our Mission

To deliver superior mechanical engineering and construction services with the highest standards of quality, safety, and professionalism. We are committed to building lasting infrastructure that supports India's industrial growth while creating value for our clients, employees, and communities.

02

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted and preferred engineering construction company, recognized for our technical excellence, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to client satisfaction. We envision expanding our footprint across all major industrial sectors in India and beyond.

03

Our Objective

To continuously enhance our technical capabilities, expand our skilled workforce, and adopt modern construction technologies. We aim to complete every project on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards, while maintaining zero-accident safety records.
